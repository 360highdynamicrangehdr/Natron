--------

| Virtual Pixel     | vpixel        | Choice    | Transparent     | Virtual Pixel Method.                                                                                   |
+-------------------+---------------+-----------+-----------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Amplitude         | amp           | Double    | 25              | Adjust wave amplitude                                                                                   |
+-------------------+---------------+-----------+-----------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Length            | length        | Double    | 150             | Adjust wave length                                                                                      |
+-------------------+---------------+-----------+-----------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
